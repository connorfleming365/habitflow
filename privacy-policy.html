Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

The short version

Swell stores everything on your device. We don't collect your data, don't have servers that store your information, and don't share anything with third parties. Your habits are yours.

📱 What Swell collects

Swell does not collect any personal information. All data you create in the app — your habits, completion history, streaks, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (SharedPreferences).

We do not have access to this data. It never leaves your device.

🌐 Network access

Swell does not make any network requests. The app has no backend servers, no user accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics. It works entirely offline.

🔔 Notifications

If you choose to enable reminders, Swell schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are processed entirely by Android — no notification data is sent to us or any third party.

You can disable notifications at any time in the app's settings or in your phone's notification settings.

🏠 Home screen widget

The optional home screen widget reads your habit data from your device's local storage to display your progress. This data remains on your device at all times.

🤝 Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data to share. Swell does not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social login or identity providers
  • Any external APIs or services

👦 Children's privacy

Swell does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of any age.

🗑️ Deleting your data

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete it, you can use the Reset all habits option in Settings, or uninstall the app. Either action permanently removes all Swell data from your device.

🔄 Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since Swell collects no data, any future changes would only reflect new features, not new data practices.
